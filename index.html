About Us

Established in 1998, McNamara's Pub brings an authentic Irish dive bar experience to the Cudell neighborhood. Known for its warm atmosphere, rotating taps, and cozy wood-paneled charm, it’s the kind of place where regulars and first-timers alike feel right at home. Whether you’re stopping in for a pint of Guinness or catching up with friends on the patio, McNamara’s offers a laid-back, genuine pub experience.
